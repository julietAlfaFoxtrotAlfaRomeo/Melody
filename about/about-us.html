Our Main Expertise

DAKARA is a privately-owned Indonesian-based international and domestic trading company. Our network spans the Middle East area, offering a variety of products such as snacks, charcoal, and briquettes. Our third-party warehousing network in strategic locations enables us to serve customers just in time. With headquarters in Jakarta, Indonesia, and subsidiaries specializing in each product group, we've grown into a team of internationally recognized experts. We specialize in providing primary raw and semi-finished products to semi-fabricators, businesses, and downstream industries, establishing ourselves as a globally recognized industry leader by both suppliers and customers.

VISION

Our Aim is to continually exceed our customer and supplier’s expectations with premium quality and cost-effective products and services in an aim to further maintain and develop a value oriented international business. We believe in growing together and practice the principle of fair business in all our dealings and transactions.

MISSION

Our mission is to be a socially responsible and resource-efficient company offering innovative solutions to our customers and providing opportunities to our partners.
